Student Chapter of the national society IETE. This is one of the most elite technical societies of TIET, exclusively for ECE and ENC undergraduates and diploma students.

About

Focused at creating learning environments in embedded systems and robotics by organising events and workshops for members.

verified_user

Mission

To develop real life problem solving skills in electronics engineering undergrads so as to inculcate in them a strategic mindset required to face company interviews and industrial level problems in the field of Embedding Systems and Robotics.

visibility

Vision

To create Engineers who grow up to challenge realistic Embedded Systems and Robotics issues worldwide, across industries and research Universities, using their learned concepts of microcontrollers, microcomputers, Deep Learning, Computer Vision and NLP.
